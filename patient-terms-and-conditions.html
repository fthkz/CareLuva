  • You should verify important details directly with the clinic
  • CareLuva is not responsible for clinic-provided information

3.3 Appointment Booking

When booking appointments:

  • Select your preferred date and time
  • Choose a specific professional (if applicable)
  • Provide accurate contact and medical information
  • Wait for clinic confirmation
  • Receive confirmation through the platform

3.4 Cancellation Policies

Cancellation policies vary by clinic:

  • Check clinic-specific cancellation policies before booking

If you cannot attend an appointment:
